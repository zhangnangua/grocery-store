Game Box Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We collect information to provide better services. The types of information we may collect include device information, gameplay records, log data, version information, version codes, game information, gameplay status, gameplay duration, game loading times, game progress, and more.

2. Information Use

We use the collected information to maintain and improve our services, as well as to develop new services.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share personal information with outside parties except as required by law or for the purpose of providing services.

4. Access and Control of Personal Information

Users can review and update their personal information, and can request to delete or restrict the processing of their personal information.

5. Security

We strive to protect users' information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes to the Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through feedback.
